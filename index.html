A truly unqiue experience

Now looking forward to its triumphant 22nd year in January 2018, the world famous Crystal Boot Awards is staged at the Norcalympia in Blackpool, England and provides the greatest line dance spectacle

Workshops

A dozen of the world's favourite choreographers personally teach their own CBA inspired dances

Live Acts

Your favourite DJs and Live Artistes take care of your music needs all of the way through the weekend

Gala Show

The Show on Saturday night is packed with the finest live entertainement and top hosts delivering all of the awards
